## Get Current User

**Request :**

- Method : <Badge method="GET">GET</Badge>
- Endpoint : `/v1/account/me`
- Headers :
  - `Authorization: Bearer <accessToken>`
  - `Content-Type: application/json`
  - `Accept: application/json`
