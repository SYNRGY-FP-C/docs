## Get Current User `MVP`

**Request :**

- Method : <Badge method="GET">GET</Badge>
- Endpoint : `/api/users/me` or else
- Headers :
  - `Authorization: Bearer <accessToken>`
  - `Content-Type: application/json`
  - `Accept: application/json`

**Response :** <Badge outline variant="success">200 OK</Badge>
