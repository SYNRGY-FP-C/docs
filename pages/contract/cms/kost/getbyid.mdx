- Method : <Badge method="GET">GET</Badge>
- URL : `https://backend-synrgy-fp-c.zekhoi.dev`
- Endpoint : `/v1/kost/:id`
- Headers :
  - `Authorization: Bearer <access_token>`
  - `Content-Type: application/json`
  - `Accept: application/json`
