## Get Room to edit `MVP`

**Request :**

- Method : <Badge method="GET">GET</Badge>
- URL : `https://be-java-binar-fp-staging.up.railway.app`
- Endpoint : `/v1/rooms/:id` or else
- Headers :
  - `Authorization: Bearer <access_token>`
  - `Content-Type: application/json`
  - `Accept: application/json`
